Learning Notes for Estimation and Filtering

一 13 十月 2014

Filed under Pearls

Tags Notes Estimation Filtering

以下给出一些常用公式及概念的证明$x\bar$:

混合高斯均值方差证明

令$p(x)=\sum_{j=1}^n p_jN(x;\bar{x}_j,C_j)$,则有: 则有:

Mean of the mixture

\begin{equation} \begin{split} E[x] & = \bar{x} \\ &= \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} xp(x) dx \\ &= \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} x\sum_{j=1}^n p_jN(x;\bar{x}j,C_j) dx \\ &= \sum{j=1}^n p_j \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} x N(x;\bar{x}j,C_j) dx \\ &= \sum{j=1}^n p_j \bar{x}_j \end{split} \end{equation}

Co-variance of the mixture

根据$Cov(x) = E[xx^T]-E[x]E[x^T]$,我们有:

\begin{equation} \begin{split} E[xx^T] &= \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} xx^Tp(x) dx \\ &= \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} xx^T\sum_{j=1}^n p_jN(x;\bar{x}j,C_j) dx \\ &= \sum{j=1}^n p_j \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} xx^T N(x;\bar{x}j,C_j) dx \\ &= \sum{j=1}^n p_j (\bar{x}_j\bar{x}_j^T + C_j) \end{split} \end{equation}

故有: \begin{equation} \begin{split} cov(x) &= E[xx^T] - E[x]E[x^T] \\ & = \sum_{j=1}^n p_j (\bar{x}j\bar{x}_j^T + C_j) - [\sum{j=1}^n p_j \bar{x}j]^2 \\ &= \sum{j=1}^n p_jC_j + \tilde{C} \end{split} \end{equation}

其中:

\begin{equation} \begin{split} \tilde{C} &= \sum_{j=1}^n p_j \bar{x}j\bar{x}_j^T - [\sum{j=1}^n p_j \bar{x}j]^2 \\ &= \sum{j=1}^n p_j\bar{x}j\bar{x}_j^T - \bar{x}\bar{x}^T \\ &= \sum{j=1}^n p_j\bar{x}j\bar{x}_j^T - 2\sum{j=1}^n p_j \bar{x}\bar{x}j^T + \sum{j=1}^n p_j\bar{x}\bar{x}^T \\ &= \sum_{j=1}^n (\bar{x}_j-\bar{x})(\bar{x}_j-\bar{x})^T p_j \end{split} \end{equation}
